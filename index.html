Welcome toNational Service Scheme

The National Service Scheme (NSS) is a fundamental sector Scheme of the Government of India, Ministry of Youth Affairs & Sports, New Delhi. Popularly known as NSS, the scheme was launched in Gandhiji’s Centenary year in 1969. NSS is a voluntary association of youth in Colleges, Universities and at the +2 level, to take part in various government-led community service activities & programmes, from time to time. The sole objective of the NSS is to provide hands-on training experience to youth in delivering community service. Read More
